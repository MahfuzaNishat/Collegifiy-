Our Story

Collegify+ was born from a simple idea in a late-night library session: what if there was an easier way for students to buy, sell, and trade essentials without breaking the bank? We were tired of overpriced textbooks and the hassle of getting rid of stuff at the end of the semester. So, we built the solution we wished we had... a marketplace for students, by students.
